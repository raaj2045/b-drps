Only bounded metadata is persisted on-chain for each paper — fixed
character fields (author, title, abstract, and review comments) together
with the IPFS content-identifier link of the manuscript, which itself
resides off-chain — yielding a constant per-paper footprint of
approximately 3.0 KiB (six copies retained across the pipeline's archival
arrays) and hence strictly linear growth in total storage, independent of
manuscript size. The slot-level accounting underlying the figure was
validated against an SSTORE count obtained via debug_traceTransaction,
with exact agreement (123 = 123 slots per publication lifecycle).
