Each paper stores only bounded metadata on-chain — short character strings
(author, title, ~70-byte abstract, review comments) plus the manuscript's
IPFS content-hash link, with the PDF itself kept off-chain — so the
per-paper footprint is a constant ~3.0 KiB (six retained copies across the
pipeline's archive arrays) and total storage grows linearly in N,
independent of manuscript size. The slot accounting behind the figure was
validated exactly against a debug_traceTransaction SSTORE count
(123 = 123 slots for one publication lifecycle).
